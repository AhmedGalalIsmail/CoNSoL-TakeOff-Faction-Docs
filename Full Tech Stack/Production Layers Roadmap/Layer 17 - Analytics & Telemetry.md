# Layer 17 - Analytics & Telemetry
^lyr17

## Purpose

Measure and monitor system usage, business outcomes, and operational health to enable informed decisions.

---

## AI Agent Mission

Act as a Data & Observability Engineer responsible for:

- Collecting product and business metrics
- Collecting operational telemetry
- Building dashboards
- Enabling observability for performance, security, and business KPIs

---

## Objectives

- Track feature usage and adoption
- Monitor system performance
- Detect anomalies and trends
- Provide actionable insights
- Support data-driven decisions

---

## Required Deliverables

- Event tracking plan
- Telemetry instrumentation
- Dashboards for operations and business metrics
- Reports and alerts for anomalies
- KPI documentation

---

## Execution Tasks

### Task Group 01 - Product Analytics

Agent must:

- Instrument features for usage tracking
- Track funnels and retention
- Provide adoption metrics

Validation:

- Metrics accurately collected
- Dashboards reflect real usage

---

### Task Group 02 - Operational Telemetry

Agent must:

- Collect logs, metrics, traces
- Monitor uptime, latency, errors
- Track resource utilization

Validation:

- Observability complete
- Alerts configured for critical events

---

### Task Group 03 - Data Analysis & Reporting

Agent must:

- Generate executive dashboards
- Produce operational reports
- Provide business insights

Validation:

- Reports accurate
- Actionable recommendations provided

---

### Task Group 04 - Alerting & Automation

Agent must:

- Define thresholds for KPIs
- Automate alerts for deviations
- Integrate with incident response

Validation:

- Alerts tested and actionable
- Anomalies addressed timely

---

## Anti-Patterns

- Incomplete telemetry
- Missing dashboards
- No actionable insights
- Delayed alerts

---

## Success Criteria

- Metrics reliably collected
- Dashboards actionable
- Anomalies detected promptly
- Business and operational decisions supported

---
